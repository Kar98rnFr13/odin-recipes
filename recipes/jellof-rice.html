Jellof Rice

A step-by-step decription on how to prepare jellof rice

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Maggi
  • Fish
  • Pepper
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Boil water in pot
  2. Add the measured quantity rice.Put down after 5mins
  3. Put an empty pot on cooker and pour in oil
  4. Add mashed onion and pepper and fry for 3 minutes
  5. pour in boiled rice and allow to boil
  6. Bring down after about 20-30mins
  7. Enjoy
