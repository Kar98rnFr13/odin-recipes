Noodles Recipe

Jellof Rice

A step-by-step guide on how to prepare basic noodles

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Noodles
  • Pepper
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Boil water in pot and wait for it to boil
  2. Tear open the packet of noodles and put inside boiling water
  3. Tear open the spices and pour into pot
  4. Add mashed onion and pepper and cook for about 5-10mins
  5. Bring down and enjoy
